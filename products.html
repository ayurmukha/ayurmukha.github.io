About AyurMukha

AyurMukha is a woman-led ayurvedic cosmetic clinic founded in 2020 by Dr. Devika Joby with a prime goal to provide ayurvedic and natural solutions to cosmetics problems faced by its customers. The clinic provides 100% customized products manufactured with atmost care. We believe that same product may not be adequate for every individual, rather, we should be able to customize and address problems faced by each individual. AyurMukha's products contain only natural ingredients making it safe to use. No chemicals are included in the medicines. We have strict quality checks and all products are customized as per the needs of the customer and their body conditions and allergies. We believe in providing solutions that are 100% natural and adapted to the user's body. Usually most beauty products are made in bulk in plants. But, does this ensure it'll give 100% results for your skin type and body features? AyurMukha's products are custom made, after a continous and rigorous routine of information gathering by the doctors themselves, from each customer. Each container of an AyurMukha product is custom made to fit the conditions of the customer's skin and body features. This requires each ingredient to be selected and processed with care and a need to keep a strict check on quantity and quality. We do not bulk manufacture any of the products. Yes this might delay the delivery but what is better than a trustable product? We sent you the products. Now what? We do not dispatch something and leave it there. We'll be checking with you once in a while to ensure the products work for you and if needed, we'll make improvements depending on the results in the next batch of products you order. What's better than a very good feedback loop?
